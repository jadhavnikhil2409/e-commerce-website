About Shoes

Shoes are a type of footwear that protect and comfort the human foot. They are worn on the feet, usually in pairs, and are often worn with socks. .



FAQS

Shoes are important because they protect your feet, prevent injuries, and can improve your performance. .

Customers consider comfort, fit, and quality when buying shoes.

Shoes that are too big or too small can cause your heels to slip. Worn-out heel counters can also cause slippage.
